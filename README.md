# NCBS Bioinformatics Internship

## Overview
This repository contains my bioinformatics internship work focused on evolutionary and structural analysis of RNA Polymerase-associated proteins.

## Research Projects

### 1. Comparative Evolutionary Analysis of σ⁷⁰ [rpoD]

Conducted comparative evolutionary analysis of the σ⁷⁰ [rpoD] subunit across Lactobacillus strains to identify sequence-level variations suggestive of horizontal gene transfer events.

### 2. Conserved Domain Analysis of RPB1

Characterized conserved domains within the RPB1 subunit of RNA Polymerase II across diverse eukaryotic phyla to study structural conservation and functional stability.

### 3. Cross-Kingdom RNA Polymerase II Comparison

Performed comparative analysis between Animalia and Plantae RNA Polymerase II subunits to evaluate evolutionary divergence and lineage-specific adaptations.

## Techniques Used
- Sequence Alignment
- Domain Mapping
- Structural Prediction
- Comparative Analysis
- Evolutionary Interpretation

## Skills Developed
Bioinformatics, Comparative Genomics, Evolutionary Biology, Sequence Analysis, Structural Biology
