Comparative Analysis Performed

1. Cross-strain comparison of σ⁷⁰ [rpoD] subunits across Lactobacillus strains to identify sequence variation and possible horizontal gene transfer signatures.

2. Cross-phyla comparison of RPB1 domains in RNA Polymerase II to evaluate structural conservation.

3. Cross-kingdom comparison between Animalia and Plantae RNA Polymerase II subunits to study evolutionary divergence and lineage-specific adaptations.
